    MAGAZINE PROJECT

    The objective of this initiative is to create a magazine dedicated to the artist CL. The design layout aims to embody her distinctive and innovative music and art style, characterized by its unique, experimental and polished nature. By utilizing a restricted color scheme, I have succeeded in crafting a contemporary and refined aesthetic for the magazine that is both sleek and dynamic.

    ICON PROJECT

    The focal point of this icon project is to portray a significant wild animal indigenous to various designated nations. Through the process of simplification, we have successfully captured the essence of the animal's most exceptional attributes, producing flat icons that are both visually appealing and easily recognizable. A restrained color palette, consisting of 5 to 6 colors, has been employed in the creation of each icon, resulting in a harmonious color scheme. Additionally, the application of the golden ratio and a circular frame has been utilized to create a cohesive and uniform appearance for the entire set of icons.

    BRANDING PROJECT - PTERON

    The Petron branding initiative encompasses the creation of a sports clothing line, aptly named 'Pteron,' meaning wing in Greek, a concept that aligns perfectly with our brand's ethos of dynamism, energy, motivation, strength, and consistency. In pursuit of a strong visual identity, we employed a sleek and geometric typography, 'Movement,' that complemented the brand's aesthetic. The color scheme utilized was restricted, serving to reinforce the brand's unique identity and promote a sense of cohesion across all design elements.

    3D ANIMATION - PTERON

    As an extension of the Pteron project, I have produced a concise yet visually compelling animation featuring a 3D product model of Pteron. The color scheme employed aligns with the S/S season.

    APP PROJECT - UNICORN

    Unicorn is a mobile application that aims to enhance the social and academic experiences of college and university students. The application offers a range of essential features, including facilitating meet-ups among students who share similar interests and organizing group study sessions. Additionally, users are notified of upcoming events and encouraged to engage in gamification activities that incentivize posting, commenting, and socializing. The application's greenish color scheme was specifically chosen to cultivate a warm and welcoming ambiance.

    MR.TONKATSU PROJECT

    This animation project pertains to Mr. Tonkatsu, an established Japanese pork cutlet restaurant located in Toronto. My task involved creating a straightforward promotional animation for their new menu, intended for possible launch in their Wellesley location. Despite being a Japanese restaurant, the establishment prides itself on a modern ambiance, deviating from traditional Japanese aesthetics. The new sauces introduced are inspired by western-style sauces commonly associated with American family restaurants, as opposed to authentic Japanese cuisine. To complement the aforementioned aspects, I employed a retro design approach for the animation, ensuring a cohesive visual narrative.

    UNIQLO LOGO REVEAL PROJECT

    As a personal project, I developed a simplistic logo reveal animation for Uniqlo. Given that Uniqlo's brand identity is grounded in minimalism, the animation was crafted with the intention of embodying the company's core values. The animation serves as a visual representation of Uniqlo's personality, conveying the brand's strength and unique identity through its clean, straightforward design.

    MOTION DESIGN PACKAGE PROJECT

This project allowed me to showcase my skills in motion design and demonstrate my ability to create visually captivating and engaging content for sports-related applications.

    STREAMING SERVICE WEB PROJECT - MELON

The project prioritizes accessibility, ensuring that users can easily navigate the website on multiple devices and platforms. The implementation of Flexbox technology has enabled me to achieve a clean, organized interface, enhancing the user experience and ultimately leading to increased engagement with the website.

    BOOTSTRAP PROJECT

    GRID PROJECT

    I have crafted an inclusive and accessible web page, leveraging the dynamic capabilities of Grid and SASS technologies. By utilizing Grid technology, I have been able to achieve an organized, responsive layout that adapts to the user's device and ensures seamless navigation. Furthermore, the implementation of SASS has facilitated efficient and effective styling of the page, contributing to its aesthetic appeal and overall user experience. By prioritizing accessibility, I have developed a web page that caters to diverse user needs, ensuring that all individuals can engage with the content effectively.
